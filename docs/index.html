About DJ-VJ

  • The DJ-VJ app is a usefull tool that anyone can utilize to have a unique visual show that will react to whatever is going on in the room. You can make a set of rules, called a Moment, for when the app should change to each video you have selected. The different variables the app can account for that will change the video are pitch, tempo, and time. This app is usefull in professional settings, and in casual environments.
  • For example:
  • Say you have a concert with music that doesn't have an exact time length and is more of an imporv show. This app will let you have visual effects that you change based on how the music changes during the performance.
  • A more casual way you can use this app is by livening up your at home party with some cool changing visuals to entertain your guest.

    • How to Use DJ-VJ

  • Using the DJ-VJ app is very simple and user friendly.
  • After opening the app using the executable, you then click the "create show" button. Here it will prompt you to pick the first video you want to use. You will browse your desktop to find the first video you want to use and select it.
  • Then, you can add whatever rules you'd like for this video involving the pitch, tempo, and time. You can set them greater than, less than, or equal to a value you then enter in the far right.
  • When you are done with this rule, you can click the "add rule" button and add it. You can also remove rules you don't want to use anymore and add other moments.
  • Once you're done making your show, you click the create show button and you will name your DJ-VJ show file.
  • You can then go back to the main screen and click the "load show" button. You will then be prompted to click the show you just created.
  • You will then get a prompt that will tell you the rules and moments you have for this show. It will also tell you how you can pause and kill your show using the "p" and "k" buttons on your keyboard.
  • From here your show will play until you kill it by pressing the "k" key on your device.
